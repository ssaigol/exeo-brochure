Meet the Partners

Francis
Tourigny

B.C.L., QUÉBEC BAR

Francis has been practicing law in the immigrant investor industry since 2009, with a focus on Canadian and U.S. business immigration law. He assists investors, entrepreneurs, workers, and multinational companies throughout the process of obtaining visas, work permits, and permanent residency.

Before co-founding Exeo, Francis worked for one of the leading business immigration firms in Hong Kong, where he helped hundreds of individuals emigrate to Canada and the United States. In addition to representing numerous clients, Francis regularly travels to the United States, Europe, Latin America, and Asia, where he gives lectures on business immigration topics.

Francis began his career at two of Canada’s most prestigious law firms. He holds a law degree from the University of Montreal and the Université Paris-1 Panthéon-Sorbonne. He also has a bachelor’s degree in sociology and political science from the University of Montreal.

Marc-André
Séguin

B.C.L., LL.B, LL.M., QUÉBEC BAR, NEW YORK STATE BAR

Marc-André has been practicing law in the immigrant investor industry since 2010, focusing on U.S. and Canadian business immigration law. He is a frequent international speaker on Canadian and U.S. immigration law topics and is often invited to comment on these issues in media and publications worldwide.

He has published a widely-read manual on the Quebec Immigrant Investor Program and has developed expertise in corporate immigration compliance matters. Additionally, he is the founding president of For the Refugees, an internationally recognized NGO based in Canada, and serves as the Vice President of the Canada-Florida Chamber of Commerce.

Marc-André has personally developed business relationships with professionals in various industries across more than 20 countries and maintains a regular presence in Latin America and Asia. He is fluent in English, Spanish, and French, and has a working knowledge of Portuguese.
