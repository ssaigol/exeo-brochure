Canada Immigration Programs

Start-Up Visa Program

This program grants permanent residency to innovative entrepreneurs who can build businesses with high growth potential in Canada. Candidates must secure funding or support from designated venture capital firms, angel investors, or business incubators.

    Key Benefits:

  • No minimum personal investment required
  • Direct permanent residency (PR) upon approval
  • Opportunity to scale a business in Canada’s global marketplace
  • Access to government-backed support networks, incubators, and funding sources
  • Spouse and dependents can also obtain PR and benefit from work/study rights

Quebec Entrepreneur
Immigration Program

This program is designed for experienced entrepreneurs who wish to establish or acquire a business in Quebec. The program has two streams: one for entrepreneurs with the support of a business incubator, accelerator, or university-affiliated program, and another for those who wish to create or acquire a business with their own capital.

    Key Benefits:

  • Provides a clear pathway to permanent residency
  • Integration into Quebec’s dynamic and diverse business ecosystem
  • Support for expanding businesses in local and international markets
  • Access to financial and business development support through government initiatives
