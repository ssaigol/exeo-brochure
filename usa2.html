US Immigration Programs

EB-1C Program

Multinational Executives & Managers

The EB-1C visa is tailored for executives and managers transferring to a U.S. branch, subsidiary, or affiliate of their company. It offers an expedited path to a green card without the need for labor certification. Applicants must have worked for the company for at least one year in the past three years before transferring to the U.S.

    Key Benefits:

  • No labor certification required
  • Expedited green card process
  • Ideal for multinational corporations expanding to the U.S.
  • Pathway to U.S. citizenship for long-term residents

L-1A Program

Intracompany Transferee Executive or Manager

The L-1A visa allows international companies to transfer executives or managers to their U.S. office, making it an ideal option for business expansion. It enables foreign businesses to open new U.S. subsidiaries or send key personnel to manage an existing one. This visa is valid for up to 7 years and can lead to permanent residency under the EB-1C category.

    Key Benefits:

  • Pathway to permanent residency (EB-1C)
  • Spouses eligible for work authorization
  • Valid up to 7 years
  • Facilitates global business operations
