US Immigration Programs

EB-5 Program

Offers wealthy investors a simple and hassle-free
path to the green card.

The U.S. EB-5 Program offers high-net-worth investors a straightforward and hassle-free path to obtaining a green card. The program requires the applicant to make a minimum investment of USD $800,000 in a Targeted Employment Area (TEA) or USD $1,050,000 in other qualifying businesses, and create at least ten full-time jobs for U.S. citizens or permanent residents.

The program grants conditional permanent residency within two years, meaning the investor, their spouse, and dependent children under 21 begin to enjoy all the rights and privileges available to green card holders in just two years.

Successful applicants benefit from the right to live, work, study, or even retire in the U.S.. The applicants' children can study as in-state students, secure employment, or start a new business without the restrictions that apply to foreign students.

  • Fast-track to U.S. permanent residency
  • Family members (spouse & children under 21) included
  • No day-to-day management requirment for investors
  • Access to U.S. business opportunities and markets
  • Eligible to apply for U.S. citizenship after five years of residency
