US Immigration Programs

EB-1A Program

Extraordinary Ability

The EB-1A visa is designed for individuals with extraordinary ability in sciences, arts, education, business, or athletics, demonstrated through sustained national or international acclaim. This visa does not require a job offer, allowing for self-petition.

    Key Benefits:

  • No job offer required (self-petition possible)
  • Priority processing for eligible applicants
  • Direct path to a green card
  • Ideal for highly accomplished professionals, artists, entrepreneurs

O-1 Program

Individuals with Extraordinary Ability

The O-1 visa is reserved for individuals with extraordinary ability in sciences, education, business, athletics, or the arts, recognized through national or international awards, major achievements, and industry contributions. Unlike the EB-1A, the O-1 does not directly lead to a green card, but it is renewable indefinitely for qualifying individuals.

    Key Benefits:

  • Renewable indefinitely
  • No labor certification required
  • Recognition by national/international awards
  • Ideal for top-tier professionals, artists, and scientists
