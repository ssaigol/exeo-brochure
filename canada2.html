Canada Immigration Programs

Quebec Self-Employed
Immigration Program

This program is ideal for individuals in cultural, tech, artistic, athletic, or professional fields who want to continue their self-employed activities in Quebec while obtaining permanent residency. Unlike the federal self-employed program, Quebec’s version requires applicants to prove financial self-sufficiency and a minimum settlement fund.

    Key Benefits:

  • No minimum investment required beyond settlement funds
  • Permanent residency eligibility based on professional experience and economic contribution
  • Access to Quebec’s extensive professional and cultural networks
  • The ability to operate as an independent worker without needing an employer

Quebec Immigrant
Investor Program

This program is designed for high-net-worth individuals who want to obtain permanent residency in Quebec through a significant financial investment. It allows investors to secure PR by making a five-year, CAD $1.2 million investment in a government-approved financial institution.

    Key Benefits:

  • Permanent residency for the investor and immediate family members
  • No requirement for active business management
  • Secure investment in a government-administered fund
  • Access to Quebec’s stable and growing economy
  • A passive investment model with no operational obligations
