Talent Passport

Passeport Talent

A long-term residence permit designed to attract highly skilled professionals, entrepreneurs, researchers, and investors to France. It is part of France’s initiative to strengthen its economy through foreign expertise.

    Key Benefits:

  • Residence permit valid for up to 4 years, renewable
  • Provides a direct path to long-term residency or French citizenship
  • Family members receive residence permits with work authorization
  • Access to France’s thriving business, research, and startup ecosystem
