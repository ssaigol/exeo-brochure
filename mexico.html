Mexico Immigration Programs

FM2 Residency

Permanent by Economic Solvency

This option grants permanent residency to individuals with substantial financial means, enabling them to live in Mexico without employment obligations.

    Key Benefits:

  • No need to work or invest in a business
  • Full residency rights for spouses and dependents
  • Direct path to Mexican citizenship after 5 years
  • Right to own property and establish businesses without needing a local partner

FM2 Residency

Temporary by Economic Solvency

Designed for retirees, remote workers, and professionals seeking temporary residency in Mexico, this permit is renewable and can lead to permanent residency.

    Key Benefits:

  • Valid for up to 4 years, with an option to apply for permanent residency
  • No business investment required
  • Allows extended stays for digital nomads, retirees, and independent professionals
  • Ability to sponsor dependents
